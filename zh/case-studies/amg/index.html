项目一：搭建 AMG 社媒内容体系

  • 先调研 agency 的市场定位，再从零梳理内容现状（我入职的时候，完全没有可持续的社媒运营体系）
  • 设计了三大内容支柱框架：固定栏目包括幕后花絮、艺术家专访、每周精选，保证内容节奏感；同时配套完整的话题标签和发布节奏策略，全部基于充分调研和竞品对标。
  • 我自己新增了第四大支柱：Expert Insights 专家洞察——一个思想领导力访谈系列。四个主题方向和所有访谈问题都是我产出的，够 16 周的原创内容，视频也由我制作。
  • 基于团队提供的素材，我负责 AMG 自有账号的内容撰写和排期发布。

Expert Insights Series — Discussion Questions

Track 1 — Intentional Branding for Artists

Week 1: From Personal Style to Brand Identity

  1. Artists often say, "I just post what feels right." At what point does that stop being enough?
  2. How do you help artists define core brand elements, such as voice, visuals, values?
  3. What's the difference between personal taste and brand identity — and how do you find the overlap that feels authentic to the artist?

Week 2: From Talent to Positioning

  1. A lot of artists are talented but still struggle to be remembered. In your experience, what's usually missing?
  2. Beyond making good music, how does clear positioning change the kinds of opportunities an artist attracts?
  3. How do you find what makes an artist distinct when their genre is crowded?

Week 3: Consistency Without Losing Creative Freedom

  1. How do you think about consistency as something that makes it easier for people to recognize an artist?
  2. What parts of an artist's brand need to stay consistent — and what parts can evolve naturally over time?
  3. How do you keep brand consistency when there are multiple collaborators (manager/label, videographer, stylist, PR)?

Week 4: Recap Reel

  1. If you had to sum up intentional branding for artists in one sentence, what is it?
  2. When an artist reaches out for branding help, what are the first things you want to learn or review?
Track 2 — The Cross-Section Between PR and Social Impact

Week 1: Turning Values Into Action

  1. When an artist comes to you with a social impact idea they really care about, how do you help turn it into a real plan?
  2. What are some concrete steps they can take to get started?
  3. How do you help the artist decide what level of impact work makes sense for their current stage and schedule?

Week 2: From Giving Back to Building Narrative

  1. How does having an ongoing initiative or nonprofit shape how people see an artist beyond their music?
  2. What kinds of stories or moments do you look for when turning impact work into PR without forcing it?
  3. What are some common mistakes artists make when sharing impact work publicly?

Week 3: Why Social Impact Is a Long-Term PR Asset

  1. How does impact work support an artist's PR in a different way than traditional PR does?
  2. Over time, how does consistent impact work influence how media or industry partners talk about an artist?
  3. How do you keep impact work consistent when an artist is busy, touring, or in album mode?

Week 4: Recap Reel

  1. What are the 3 things an artist needs to get right if they want impact work to support their brand and PR long-term?
  2. What does success look like when impact work and PR are working well together over time?
Track 3 — Direct-to-Fan Growth for Artists

Week 1: What Direct-to-Fan Really Means (and Why It's Rising)

  1. When you say "direct-to-fan," what do you mean in simple terms?
  2. Why are more artists talking about direct-to-fan right now? What is changing in the industry?
  3. What problems does direct-to-fan solve that social platforms do not solve?

Week 2: Building a Fan Offer People Will Pay For

  1. What makes fans willing to pay? What are they really paying for?
  2. What are some examples of "exclusive content" that are realistic to create on a regular schedule?
  3. What are the most common mistakes artists make when launching a paid fan offer?

Week 3: Keeping It Alive (Retention, Community, and Consistency)

  1. A lot of artists can launch something, but keeping it going is harder. How do you keep direct-to-fan consistent over time?
  2. What role does community play in retention? How do you get fans to interact with each other, not just consume content?
  3. How do you keep the exclusive space feeling special without putting everything behind a paywall?

Week 4: Recap Reel

  1. If an artist is deciding whether to start, what is the gut-check question they should ask themselves first?
  2. What are some things an artist needs to get right to build a direct-to-fan channel that lasts?
Track 4 — Experiential Marketing for Artists

Week 1: Why Experiences Matter (and How to Find the Right Fit)

  1. Why are experiences so powerful for artists? What can an in-person moment do that online content cannot?
  2. How do you figure out what kind of experience fits an artist best (concert, livehouse, nightclub, exhibition, listening party, pop-up)?
  3. What's a good starter experience for an artist who wants to try this but doesn't want to take a big risk?

Week 2: Making It Worth It

  1. Before you plan anything, how do you define what "success" looks like for an experience?
  2. What are realistic ways experiences can create value for the artist?
  3. How do experiences support an artist's PR? What kinds of media angles or interests can they create?

Week 3: Turning Fans Into Community

  1. What makes an experience memorable for fans? What details create that "I was there" feeling?
  2. How do you make fans feel seen and included during the experience?
  3. What are some simple ways to design the experience so fans naturally want to share it online?

Week 4: Recap Reel

  1. After the event, how do you keep the momentum going so it doesn't end after one night?
  2. What's the biggest mistake you wish artists would stop making when it comes to live experiences?

项目二：转型扩展：适配 LinkedIn 重做内容体系

  • 专家洞察系列数据没起来：内容做得很扎实，但 Instagram 的表现配不上这个系列的质量。
  • 把同一套内容支柱体系重新适配新平台和新受众：周一行业干货、周三专家洞察（改成 B2B 语气）、周五客户成果汇总
  • 规划了三阶段上线节奏：先打基础建信任，再稳定更新 + 定向触达，最后做长文思想领导力内容。
  • 这套策略是我离职前设计的，没等到完全落地看到效果。

第一阶段：基础搭建

第 1-4 周：完善公司主页、关于板块；从 Instagram 高赞内容改编 5 条帖子首发。

2

第二阶段：稳定更新

第 5-12 周：三大内容支柱每周稳定更新，每周 3 条；同时定向触达经纪公司管理者、A&R 和独立音乐人。

3

第三阶段：思想领导力

双周发布长文内容——案例拆解和行业观点——根据主页访问数据持续优化方向。

项目三：用 Notion 重构公司内部运营系统

  • 重新设计了整个公司的内部工作系统
  • 不止是重构，我还规划了更深层的自动化和 AI 整合（自动同步会议纪要、Slack 双向同步、自动归档、跨数据库客户视图、邮件自动入库等）
  • 系统一上线便投入使用，使所有工作都能标准化复用

客户项目

除了 AMG 自有社媒运营，我还全程负责另外 10+ 客户的内容运营和物料制作，覆盖音乐、公益、法律、消费品牌等多个领域。

无论是对内还是对外，把体系搭对了，内容自然就顺了。
